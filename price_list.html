Cancellation Policy

  • If cancellation is made more than 28 days ahead of the time of boarding, no payment is due.
  • Cancellations with less than 28 days notice will be invoiced at 50% of the total cost of the stay.
  • Cancellations with less than 7 days notice will be invoiced at the full rate.

We will endeavour to fill any dates of cancelled bookings, and in such situations, any sums paid will be transferred once, to a later booking- where possible. (Please check with us for availability before re-booking your holiday).

Regular daycare/walking slots require 7 days notice of cancellation (unless your dog is unwell and in need of veterinary treatment) Cancellations without the required notice will be charged at usual rate.
