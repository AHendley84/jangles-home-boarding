About

Jangles Home Boarding, Walking & Daycare is a joint venture run by Cath and Dave Jones.

Established in 2014, we take great pride in offering a home from home experience for our guests and go the extra mile to ensure that your dog has the best time possible during their stay.

Each of our guests can relax on a variety of dog beds and sofas, or for those who prefer their own space, we are happy to offer separate rooms. We have a large, totally secure outdoor area. Our guests are free to play and relax both indoors and out.

All daycare and boarding guests are exercised in accordance with owner’s wishes as far as possible. We offer a variety of group walks, to local woodlands, parks and secure fields.

Meet The Team

Resident Dogs

Mr Beau Jangles Jones, English Pointer - CEO

Born in 2012, Mr Beau Jangles Jones is the founding member of Team Jangles and the original CEO.

With a penchant for cheese (he can sense the opening of a cheese wrapper from a mile off!) he’s a complex soul, expecting high standards of behaviour at Jangles HQ (from the humans as well as canine friends).

Nicknames: Bubba; Twinkle Toe; Trotting Trevor; Sad Sack; Captain Sensible; Sergeant FunPolice.

Favourite Thing in the world: A tennis ball (and cheese!).

Mac the Knife, English Pointer - Assistant CEO, Social Events Coordinator

Born in 2013, Mac was quite possibly the naughtiest puppy ever! (Think 4 leather sofas, 7 Sky remotes and goodness knows how many dog beds) And then he turned two, and almost overnight, became an absolute superstar.

Giddy, funny, happy, and a little bit bonkers, he really is the ideal friend for all our Jangles guests. Imagine a Butlins Redcoat in canine form.

Nicknames: Mackintosh; Wonky Donkey, Windmill Tail, Batman

Favourite Thing in the world: ripping a hole in a blanket and wearing it like a cape while running around the house at 100mph.

Joe Strummer, English Pointer

Born in 2016, Joe Strummer was an “accident”. Dave said “no more dogs”, Cath heard “just one more”, and that was that!

Joseph has many talents- we just don’t know what they are yet! In his defence, he’s shown great skills in finding lost dogs, and is also a donor for Pet Blood Bank UK. But he’s the one who was sent to try us, something he does EXTREMELY well!

Nicknames: Jo-Bee; Doofus; JoJo; Joe-Joooooe-Joseph!! (And quite a few more than can’t be published!).

Favourite Thing in the world: Birds. And Birds. And more birds. And if he can’t find a bird, a bumble bee or even a gnat will do.

Eddie Cochran, Pug / Jack Russell Cross

Also born in 2016, Eddie is the fourth wheel of the Jangles Machine. What he lacks in stature, he makes up for in personality and attitude.

Ed packs a lot into a a small (ish) body, mainly a whole heap of feistiness. Purely here for comedy value, he may be the smallest member of the Team, but he’s the loudest, and most definitely the most trouble!

Nicknames: Edward; ThugPug; Spud; Pugtato; Dyson (you need to see him clean the floors!).

Favourite Thing in the world: Food, and yelling for food.
